    Sign Language Becomes Universal

    Your evolutionary companion, grow with your child. It translates sign language into sound and vice versa, converts sounds into movements or text. Fluent communication for deaf-mutes.

    Vision

    SignoGlove's vision is to radically transform the communication experience for deaf-mute people, opening up a world of possibilities for inclusion and active participation in society. Our goal is to remove language barriers and facilitate fluid and natural communication, allowing deaf-mute people to express themselves and be understood in any environment.
